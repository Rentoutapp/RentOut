## 8. Contact Information Gating — The Unlock System

A core privacy feature of the RentOut Platform is the **Unlock System**, which protects the contact details of property Providers from public exposure:

- Property listings are publicly visible to all authenticated Tenants, but the **contact phone number** of the Provider (and agent/brokerage contact details where applicable) is **hidden** by default.
- To access contact details, a Tenant must pay a **non-refundable Unlock fee of USD $10** per property.
- Upon successful payment verification by our backend (Firebase Cloud Functions), an **Unlock record** is permanently created linking the Tenant to that property.
- The Tenant then gains access to the Provider's contact details within the app, and can also view map directions to the property.
- The Unlock is **permanent** — once unlocked, the Tenant retains access to the contact details even if the listing is later updated.
- The Provider receives a notification that one of their properties has been unlocked (but is not told which specific Tenant unlocked it at the app level).
- For **Brokerage** listings, an additional commission (15% of the unlock fee by default) is deducted from the Brokerage's float balance at the time of unlock.

This system ensures that Provider contact information is only accessible to users who have demonstrated genuine intent by completing a payment, protecting Providers from spam and unsolicited contact.

## 9. Data Storage, Security & Infrastructure

### 9.1 Where Data Is Stored
All Platform data is stored in **Google Cloud Firestore** (a NoSQL cloud database) and **Firebase Storage** (for images and files). Firebase services operate on Google Cloud infrastructure, with data centres located in the United States and potentially other Google Cloud regions. See Section 13 for information on international data transfers.

### 9.2 Security Measures
We implement the following technical and organisational security measures:

- **Firebase Authentication** — all passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms; we never store plaintext passwords.
- **Firestore Security Rules** — granular server-side rules enforce that:
  - Users can only read and write their own data.
  - Tenants cannot access contact details without a valid Unlock record.
  - Transaction and Unlock records can only be created by our Cloud Functions (server-side), never by clients.
  - Admin-only fields (such as listing status, float balances, and account suspension status) cannot be modified by regular users.
- **HTTPS / TLS** — all data in transit between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS.
- **Firebase Storage rules** — image files are protected and only accessible to authenticated users.
- **Role-based access control** — enforced at both the app layer and the Firestore rules layer; Admin, Provider, and Tenant roles each have strictly defined permissions.
- **Cloud Functions** — all financial operations (payment verification, unlock creation, float deductions) are performed server-side in a trusted environment, not on user devices.

### 9.3 Limitations
No security system is perfect. While we take all reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users as required by law.

## 27. Brokerage Float & Insurance System

### 27.1 Purpose of the Float
To participate in the Unlock system, Brokerages must maintain an **insurance float** — a pre-funded balance held on the Platform. This float serves as:
- A security deposit demonstrating the Brokerage's financial commitment to the Platform.
- A source from which commissions are automatically deducted on each Unlock.

### 27.2 Float Parameters
The following parameters apply to the Brokerage float system (subject to change by RentOut):

| Parameter | Default Value |
|-----------|--------------|
| Subscription / Initial Float Fee | USD $100.00 |
| Minimum Float Safety Threshold | USD $40.00 |
| Commission Rate per Unlock | 15% of the Unlock fee |
| Commission per standard Unlock (USD $10) | USD $1.50 |

### 27.3 Automatic Commission Deductions
Each time a Tenant unlocks a Brokerage listing, the applicable commission is automatically deducted from the Brokerage's float balance by our Cloud Functions. The Brokerage receives a ledger entry recording the deduction, including the balance before and after.

### 27.4 Float Freeze Mechanism
If a Brokerage's float balance falls **at or below the minimum safety threshold** (USD $40.00 by default), the following occurs automatically:
1. The Brokerage's account is **frozen** — no further Unlocks are permitted on their listings.
2. All of the Brokerage's listings are updated to indicate that Unlocks are temporarily unavailable.
3. Tenants attempting to unlock a frozen Brokerage's listing will be shown an appropriate message.
4. The Brokerage receives a notification.

The freeze is automatically lifted once the Brokerage tops up their float above the minimum threshold.

### 27.5 Top-Up Process
Brokerages can top up their float balance at any time through the **Brokerage Account** screen, using PesePay. Top-ups are recorded as credit entries in the Brokerage ledger and credited to the float balance upon payment confirmation.

### 27.6 Float is Non-Transferable
The Brokerage float balance is not transferable between accounts and is not withdrawable by the Brokerage. It exists solely to fund commissions on the Platform.

### 27.7 Ledger & Audit Trail
RentOut maintains a complete, immutable ledger of all float transactions (top-ups, deductions, and adjustments) for each Brokerage. This ledger is accessible to the Brokerage through the app and to RentOut Admins through the admin panel.
